About Me!

Web Developer and Programmer

I am Anurag Kaushal Yadav, pursuing a Bachelors of Engineering in Information Technology (B.E. IT) from St. Francis Institute of Technology, affiliated with Mumbai University.
Currently in my third year, I serve as the Tech Head at CSI_SFIT. My interests revolve around Web Development, Android Development, and various programming languages including C, C++, Java, Python, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL.

Apart from my academic pursuits, I indulge in hobbies such as playing cricket and badminton to maintain an active lifestyle. Additionally, I also devote time to fitness training at the gym.
I served as the Technical Head at CSI_SFIT (Computer Society of India) from August 2022 to June 2023. During my tenure, I had the privilege of being a speaker in an online JAVA workshop organized by CSI_SFIT for 1st, 2nd, and 3rd year students of SFIT, which took place on 19th March 2023.
Prior to this role, I held the position of Technical Executive at CSI_SFIT from September 2021 to July 2022. In that capacity, I conducted an offline workshop on Python, acting as a mentor for 1st, 2nd, and 3rd year students of SFIT, on 9th April 2022. These experiences allowed me to contribute to the development and knowledge sharing within the technical community at SFIT.
